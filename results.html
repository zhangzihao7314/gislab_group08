Problems and Limitations

  • Raster Negative Value
    Some cells may have negative values while generating the Susceptibility Map in step 2. If these negative values are not set to null, they can influence subsequent reclassification and resampling processes, thereby affecting the overall accuracy of the results.
  • Results Discrepancy
    Upon comparing our results with the landslide hazard risk level map from the Italian web platform on landslides and floods, we found that the actual high-risk areas are significantly smaller than those shown in our Landslide Susceptibility Map. We hypothesize that this difference is due to our consideration of an insufficient number of environmental factors and the overly simplistic definition of No Landslide Zones, which is only based on slope.
